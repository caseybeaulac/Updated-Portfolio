A Little Bit About Me

My name is Casey, I was born and raised in Montreal, Canada where I spent most of my life. After graduating high school, I decided to pursue my education at CDI College where I took the Programmer Analyst course.

After 3 years of writing lines and debugging code I finally finished the program and completed an 8 weeks internship in order to aquire the diploma as well as external experience in the field. The internship was a great way to apply what I had learned in school in a professinal matter as well as building relationships and contacts. After aquiring my diploma I decided it was time for me to explore a little bit of what the world had to offer outside of Canada. So I packed my things and drove down to Orlando where I now reside. Here I am, taking this new coding class working on improving my skills even further and potentialy launch my career as a full-stack developer. But for now I currently work as a server in a wine bar downtown Orlando trying to make the best out of it.
